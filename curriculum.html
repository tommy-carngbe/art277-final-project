High School (FMHS) Curriculum




Thought-provoking Social-Emotional Learning for High School Classrooms With web-based access and a straightforward format, teachers engage students with ease and guide them into deeper understanding of the social and emotional dynamics they navigate every day. Each lesson initiates curiosity by challenging students to reflect and discuss their First Thoughts. As lessons unfold, additional concepts, ideas, and challenges are introduced to progressively guide students toward learning, application, and practicing new skills.
